Ground Beef with Zucchini

Description:

This dish is very easy to make with a few simple ingredients and something that my mom would cook for me a lot. It might be surprising to see it topped with ketchup, but in my opinion, it gives it a nice tangy flavor from the vinegar and balances out everything.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Diced Onions
  • Minced Garlic
  • Neutral oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sliced Zucchini
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Water

Steps:

  1. In your pot, heat up oil, minced garlic, and your diced onion. Cook until the onions are soft and the garlic is fragrant.
  2. Add in your ground beef and season with anything you want. I just use salt and pepper for now.
  3. Break up the ground beef and let it cook for a bit. In the meantime, cut your zucchini.
  4. After the meat has cooked for a bit, add in your oyster sauce and stir to incorporate.
  5. Add in your zucchini slices.
  6. While the meat will release some water, add in enough water to cover the zucchini so it can get soft.
  7. Simmer until the zucchini is soft.
  8. Serve over hot rice and top with ketchup!
